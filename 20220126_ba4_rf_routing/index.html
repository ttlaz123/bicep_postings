BA4 Microwave Multiplexing Readout and RF Cable Routing Design

2022 Jan 26 — Tom Liu

To readout the detector signal, the fourth receiver of the BICEP Array (BA4) is planning to implement frequency domain multiplexing as opposed to the time domain multiplexing currently in use for the other three receivers. As a result, it is necessary to install semi-rigid coaxial cables over the previously flexible MCE cables. Here, we discuss the thermal, electrical, and mechanical constraints of our design problem and present the first iteration of the RF cable routing for the warm stages.
