The Mega Pager

We plot the pass rate, bypass rate, and failure rate for the screenings since 2019 April. To summaraize:
  • Pass: The chip produced the expected cryogenic response
  • Bypass (Warm Fail): The chip was deemed faulty during warm screening by identifying either a cross-short, an open, or an irregular resistance.
  • Cold Fail: The chip was isolated to be the case of a faulty result during cold screening
  • Unknown: The chip did not pass, but it was not isolated to be the chip at fault. Calculated by counting any chip that has a future screening date.

The screening batches are roughly divided such that each batch used one helium dewar. For screenings that took place before 2022, it is unknown how many dewars of helium were used, so they were roughly divided into when a specific wafer was received.

The relevant data that can be viewed are the pass rates, fail rates, unknown rates, and the number of times that a chip was screened. The data is divided up through wafer, screening batch, and by the number of times the chip was screened.

To select a period of time that the chips were screened, select a start date and an end date, making sure that the end date is after the start date. Each start and end date is given a name for context.
