Conclusions

Before warmup, the modulation on SSA1 was much smaller, in the 10-20 range. It can be seen that there are some double peaks around bias steps 17 - 30 in cols 8,11,12, and 13. After warming up, the double peaks went away, and the modulation returned to the expected 60-80. It's suspected that the abnormality was due to trapped flux.
